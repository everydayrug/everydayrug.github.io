Everyday Rug

Everyday Rug is a new NFT art project dedicated to all the winners and losers in crypto. Each artwork comes with a small story of fortune or pain. New work daily until we hit 6,900.

For now work is minted on the Polygon network via OpenSea. The plan is to move to the XRPL ledger and build a marketplace when the XLS-20d spec is ready.

If you would like to contribute a story please reach out on Twitter with the hashtag #EverydayRugStory, we would be happy to share the royalties or gift an NFT. Our goal is to see this become a community project.
