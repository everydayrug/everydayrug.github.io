Everyday Rug

Everyday Rug is a new NFT art project and a loving tribute to all the losers in crypto. Each artwork comes with a small story of pain and loss. New drops daily until we hit 6,900.

For now we are minting on the Polygon network via OpenSea. The plan is to migrate to the XRPL ledger and build a marketplace when xls-20 is ready.

If you would like to contribute a story please reach out on Twitter with the hashtag #EverydayRugStory, we would be happy to split the royalties. Our goal is to see this become a community effort.
